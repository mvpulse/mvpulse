# MVPulse Video Demo Script

## Video Specifications

- **Duration**: 3-5 minutes
- **Format**: Screen recording with voiceover
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p)

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Clear browser history/cache
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs
- [ ] Set browser zoom to 100%
- [ ] Prepare test wallets with tokens
- [ ] Verify Movement testnet is operational
- [ ] Test microphone levels

---

## Script

### INTRO (0:00 - 0:20)

**[SCREEN: MVPulse landing page]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "MVPulse is a decentralized polling platform built on Movement Network where users earn real token rewards for participating in polls. Let's see how it works."

---

### WALLET CONNECTION (0:20 - 0:45)

**[SCREEN: Click Connect Wallet button]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Users can connect with traditional Web3 wallets like Petra, or use our Privy integration for social login - making Web3 accessible to everyone."

**[ACTION: Connect with email/Google via Privy]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "With Privy, users get an embedded wallet automatically. On testnet, new wallets are funded with MOVE tokens for gas."

---

### CREATING A POLL (0:45 - 1:45)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Creator > Create Poll]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Creating a poll is simple. Let's create one now."

**[ACTION: Fill in poll form]**
- Title: "What's the most important feature in a blockchain?"
- Options: "Speed", "Security", "Low Fees", "Developer Experience"
- Duration: 1 hour
- Reward: 100 PULSE, Equal Split

**VOICEOVER:**
> "We choose PULSE tokens for rewards and set an equal split distribution - all voters share the reward pool equally."

**[ACTION: Create poll and confirm transaction]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "The poll is now live. The reward tokens are locked in the smart contract until distribution."

---

### VOTING ON A POLL (1:45 - 2:15)

**[SCREEN: Switch to participant view / different wallet]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Now let's switch to a participant's perspective."

**[ACTION: Browse polls and vote]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Participants browse active polls and cast votes. Each vote is recorded on-chain, ensuring transparency and immutability."

**[ACTION: Confirm vote transaction]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "After voting, participants are automatically eligible for rewards when the poll closes."

---

### QUESTIONNAIRES (2:15 - 2:45)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Questionnaires]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "MVPulse also supports questionnaires - bundled surveys with shared reward pools."

**[ACTION: Show a questionnaire with multiple polls]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Participants complete all polls in sequence and receive a share of the reward pool. All votes are recorded in a single transaction for better efficiency."

---

### STAKING & TIERS (2:45 - 3:15)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Staking page]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Users can stake PULSE tokens to unlock higher tiers. Higher tiers mean more daily votes."

**[ACTION: Show tier breakdown]**
- Bronze: 3 votes/day
- Silver: 6 votes/day
- Gold: 9 votes/day
- Platinum: 12 votes/day

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Staking also supports the platform's token economy while giving users more engagement opportunities."

---

### REFERRAL SYSTEM (3:15 - 3:35)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Referrals page]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Our referral system rewards users for inviting friends. Earn points when your referrals vote, with milestones at 1, 10, 50, and 100 votes."

**[ACTION: Show referral code and link]**

---

### TOKEN SWAP (3:35 - 3:50)

**[SCREEN: Navigate to Swap page]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Users can swap between PULSE and USDC using our built-in AMM, making token management seamless."

---

### CLOSING (3:50 - 4:15)

**[SCREEN: Return to landing page or dashboard]**

**VOICEOVER:**
> "MVPulse brings together transparent polling, token rewards, and gamification - all powered by Movement Network's fast, low-cost infrastructure.

> Visit mvpulse.xyz to try it yourself. Built for the Encode x Movement Hackathon."

**[SCREEN: Show logo, website URL, social links]**

---

## Recording Tips

- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Pause briefly between sections
- Keep mouse movements smooth
- Avoid clicking too fast
- Have backup recording ready
